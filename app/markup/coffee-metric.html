Coffee Metric

Graphic Design, UI/UX
The Problem

Learning independently about hand crafted coffee can be intimidating. There is a seemingly endless number of parameters to keep track of­ water temperature, coffee ground size, time, volume, it goes on. Learning what makes a good cup of coffee shouldn’t be as complicated as it seems.

The Solution

What started as a fun Illustrator exercise turned into an idea for a mobile and web app to help coffee newbies learn their stuff. Coffee Metric, although still in development, is an app that guides aspiring baristas in brewing coffee at home. It walks new users through the process of figuring out how much coffee or water theyneed for how much coffee they want to make. After that, the app provides a space for users to reflect on how good their cup was. Was the coffee too bitter? Too watery? Users can write down their thoughts and rate their brew, learning from their experiences in an organized way.
