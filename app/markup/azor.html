The Problem

The candle industry is one of many that is need of a branding rejuvenation. Many brands, such as Yankee Candle and Colonial Candle are no longer household names. This leaves a gap in the market for a fresh and relevant face on the candle industry.

The Solution

Azor Fragrances’ identity takes cues from today’s trends without being trendy. Trends revolving around healthy living are becoming more and more popular. The candles are made of natural ingredients, which is reflected in the company identity. The logo and print collateral mirror the simplicity of the candle components.
