Eau Naturale

Graphic Design, Photography
The Problem

With the shift to online marketplaces, there has been a growing disconnect between the user and perfumes. Computers can do a lot, but they can’t tell anyone what perfumes smell like. Scents like vetiver, patchouli and myrrh are abstract ideas to new users. Buying perfume online is a crap shoot.

The Solution

Inspired by successful subscription­based products, Eau Naturale is a service which delivers samples of seasonal perfumes to the user. The intention is to empower the user and educate them about the fragrances that go into creating a scent. Eau Naturale provides a well­rounded experience, using a variety of scents in order to expose users to the Maximum number of fragrances in the perfume world. Included in every delivery is a sample of the season’s scents and descriptions of the top, middle and base notes of each perfume. The user can then learn which scents they like or dislike, educating themselves without having to invest in a single bottle of perfume.
