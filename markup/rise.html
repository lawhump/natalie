Rise

Furniture Design, Photography
The Problem

This product was approached from a non­traditional perspective. The initial guidelines were to create functional furniture with appeal. Natural water curiosities, such as geysers and cenotes, in conjuction with modern Scandinavian design served as inspiration to the development and style of the final form.

The Solution

Completed in two and a half weeks, Rise is an ambiguous furniture piece that incpororates the wonder and aesthetic of water­based phenomena. It can function as a step stool or a shelf, allowing users to assimilate it into their daily lives as needed. Rise is made of baltic birch plywood and hand­tinted polyester resin. It is finished with polyurethane and has been sanded up to 2000 grit. The stool itself is held together using traditional mortises and tenons. By combining the preciousness of natural wonders with the simplicity of modern Scandinavian design, an emotional and sustainable attachment can be made to a simple furniture piece.
